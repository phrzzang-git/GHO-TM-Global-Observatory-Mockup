Indicator Development Roadmap

What this MDS shows now — and where it goes next
Expert consensus framing: Present missing indicators (outcome, safety, drug interaction) not as weaknesses but as the deliberate next phase — demonstrating that the MDS architecture is designed to scale.
Phase 1 · Current — Structural proof
5 Core indicators validated: OPD/IPD, MSK burden, NCD rate, Coding completeness, WM-TM Concordance
ICD-11 TM dual-coding demonstrated at 1.8M scale
Data quality flags surfaced (gender coding error)
CCI, Pareto analysis, Shannon entropy computed
Indicator definition reference published (Word doc)
Phase 2 · Next — Outcome integration
Treatment outcome field — patient-reported improvement score per visit
Adverse event / safety reporting rate — % of visits with documented adverse events
Concurrent western medication flag — drug-herb interaction risk monitoring
Revisit rate re-analysis stratified by outcome (effective retention vs. chronic necessity)
Phase 3 · Future — Comparative effectiveness
Cost-effectiveness per condition per modality
TM vs. conventional care outcome comparison for MSK & NCD
Real-world evidence synthesis across 6 countries for WHO policy brief
WM-TM Concordance methods paper — journal submission
